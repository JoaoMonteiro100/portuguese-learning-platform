Portugal

Portuguese Hymn
"A Portuguesa"

A língua

  • José Saramago is the only Portuguese with a Nobel Prize in Literature.
  • Luís de Camões and Fernando Pessoa are usually referenced as the greatest Portuguese poets of all time.
  • Like other romantic languages, it has no equivalent for "it" nor a neutral third person personal pronoun (like the Finnish "hän") and every noun has a gender (even inanimate objects).
  • Unique words: fado, saudade.
