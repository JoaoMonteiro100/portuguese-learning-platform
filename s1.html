Regras de acentuação

All words have an accentuated syllable, which is always the penultimate syllable, except if:


  • There is an accentuation sign like ´, `, ^ or ~ (and then, the syllable where it's located is the one accentuated).
    Examples: café, chá, lâmpada, armazém, coração.

  • The word ends in -l, -r, -u or -i and has no accentuation signs (the accentuation is then on the last syllable).
    Examples: farol, cantar, museu, pesquisarei.

  • It's a monosyllabic word.
    Examples: meu, pai, quem.

Mais exemplos

  • Tépido
  • Mar
  • Tempestade
  • Crepúsculo
  • Anfitrião
  • Navegar
  • Cidade
