Regras de acentuação

All words have an accentuated syllable, which is always the second-to-last syllable, except if:


  1. There is an accentuation sign like ´, `, ^ or ~ (and then, the syllable where it's located is the one accentuated).
    Examples: café, chá, lâmpada, armazém, coração.

  2. The word ends in -l, -r, -z, -u or -i and has no accentuation signs (the accentuation is then on the last syllable).
    Examples: farol, cantar, museu, pesquisarei.

  3. It's a monosyllabic word.
    Examples: meu, pai, quem.

Mais exemplos

  • Tépido
  • Mar
  • Tempestade
  • Crepúsculo
  • Anfitrião
  • Navegar
  • Cidade
  • Portugal

When it should be used:
  • When the letter has one of these accents: à or á.
  • When the letter is in an accentuated syllable.
  • When it's followed by an -r, -l, -i or -u.
      • Examples: pátria, pau, palrar.

      • Examples: cantina, irmã.

  • When the letter is in the end of a word (and has no accentuation sign).
